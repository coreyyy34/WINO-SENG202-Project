Navigating the Program

Figure 1. The WINO navigation bar when the user is logged out

Above, you can see the WINO top bar. this allows you to navigate the program. The available buttons are as follows:

  1. The Wines menu. This allows you to view wines in the database, as well as access the Compare screen.
  2. The Vineyards menu. This allows you to view information on vineyards in our database.
  3. The Social menu. This allows you to browse reviews from other users.
  4. The Help button. Opens this screen.
  5. The Login button. Allows you to log in to your account.
  6. The Register button. Allows you to register a new account.

Figure 2. The WINO navigation bar after logging in

Once you have logged the navbar changes slightly. Here are the changes:

  1. You can now access the Profile menu, which gives you access to Lists and Notes.
  2. The Login and Register buttons have been replaced with the Settings and Logout buttons.

The following sections will show you how to use the basic features of the program.

Viewing Wine Data:

You can view all wines available in the database by clicking on the 'view' option in the 'Wines' menu.

Figure 3. The 'Wines' menu.

This view can be filtered using the panel on the left. By double clicking on any of the wines shown, you will be taken to the details page, where you can see the score, reviews, and other data, as well as add that wine to your lists or take notes if you are signed in.

Signing In:

You can sign in to your account by clicking on the login button, then entering your username and password. Signing in allows you to access the note-taking and list features, leave reviews, use the consumption calculator, and access the social page. You can create an account by registering.

Registering for an account:

You can register for an account by clicking on the Register button. You will be prompted to enter your new username, then your password twice to confirm. Your password must contain at least one uppercase letter, at least one lowercase letter, at least one number, at least one symbol, and be between 8 and 30 characters long. Once you have registered, you will be logged in automatically. In the future, you can log into your account by clicking the Login button in the top right.

Account Settings

If you want to change your password, you can do so by clicking the Settings button while logged in, then clicking update password. You will be prompted to enter your old password, then the new password twice.
