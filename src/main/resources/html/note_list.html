Taking Notes

A note is always attached to a wine. You can create a new note by opening the detailed view while signed in and writing your note in the Notes section. Once you are finished, click on Save to save the note to your profile.

Managing Notes

You can view all of the notes you've created on the Notes screen. Click the Notes button on the navbar to open it. Here, you will see a list of notes you have created on the left. Double clicking on a note will open it on the right, allowing you to edit it. The 'Delete' button on this page will delete the note which is active on the right hand side of the page.

Using Lists

You can use a List to create a collection of wines that is saved to your account. This is useful for creating shopping list, remembering your favourite bottles, and more. To add a wine to a list, go to its detailed view and click on the 'Open Lists' button.

Figure X. The lists menu

Above is the Lists menu. Here, you can add a wine to a list by clicking the '+' button next to the wine, and remove by clicking the '-' button which appears in the same place.

The My Lists Tab

The My Lists tab allows you to create, edit, and delete lists. This can be opened by clicking on the 'My Lists' button on the top bar. The table on the right-hand side shows the wines present in the list, while the buttons on the left represent the lists themselves. You can create a new list by cliking 'Create List' on the sidebar, and entering a name for the new list. Afterwards, this list will be available under the 'Open Lists' menu in the detailed view. A list can be deleted by clicking on the 'Delete List' button, then confirming in the dialog. The 'Favourites' and 'History' lists are built-in and cannot be deleted.
