The Profile Menu

The Profile menu allows you to access your Notes and Lists hen you are signed in. To access these hover over the Profile option on the navbar.

Taking Notes

A note is always attached to a wine. You can create a new note by opening the detailed view while signed in and writing your note in the Notes section. Once you are finished, click on Save to save the note to your profile.

Managing Notes

Figure 1. The Notes screen

  1. The List panel. This shows a list of wines that you have token notes for.
  2. The title. When you have a note selected, its title will show up here.
  3. The editor panel. This allows you to edit your notes as well as delete them.

You can view all of the notes you've created on the Notes screen. Click the Notes button under Profile on the navbar to open it. Double clicking on a note in the list panel will open it on the right, allowing you to edit it. The 'Delete' button on this page will delete the note which is active (title is shown at the top of the page).

Using Lists

You can use a List to create a collection of wines that is saved to your account. This is useful for creating shopping list, remembering your favourite bottles, and more. To add a wine to a list, go to its detailed view and click on the 'Open Lists' button.

Figure 2. The lists menu

Above is the Lists menu. Here, you can add a wine to a list by clicking the '+' button next to the wine, and remove by clicking the '-' button which appears in the same place.

The Lists Screen

Figure 3. The Lists screen

  1. The List panel. This shows your lists, as well as the Create/Delete buttons
  2. The table panel. This shows the wines in your list.

The Lists Screen allows you to create, edit, and delete lists. This can be opened by clicking on the 'Lists' option in the profile menu. The 'Favourites' and 'History' lists are built-in and cannot be deleted. You can open a wines detailed view by double clicking on that entry
