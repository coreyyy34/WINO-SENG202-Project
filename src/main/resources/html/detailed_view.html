Overview

Figure 1. The Detailed View

The detailed view shows you more in-depth information about a wine. The features of the page, as you can see above, are:

  1. The title of the viewed wine
  2. The description panel. This shows a description of the wine.
  3. The Notes panel. this allows you to write and save notes.
  4. The button bar. This houses the Back, Save Note, Lists, and Compare buttons.
  5. The information panel. This shows various pieces of information about the wine (score, vintage, winery, etc.)
  6. The review panel. This allows you to read other users' reviews as well as leave your own.

The next section has more information on reviews.

Leaving Reviews

You can leave a review on a wine if you are logged in by clicking on the 'Add Review' button in the Reviews panel(6). From here, you can type a review text as well as choose a rating out of 5 stars. If you wish to change your review, simply come back to the detailed view and click on 'Modify Review' (this button will replace the 'Add Review' button).
