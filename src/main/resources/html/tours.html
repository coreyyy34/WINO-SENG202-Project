Tour Planning overview

You can create a new tour by clicking on the 'Create Tour button' You will then be shown the tour planning screen:

Figure 1. The Tour Planning page

  1. The Tours list. This shows all of your tours.
  2. The Available Vineyards list. This shows vineyards which are not part of your tour.
  3. The Itinerary. This shows vineyards in your current itinerary.

You can add vineyards to the tour by clicking the '+' button in the Available Vineyards list. You can remove them by clicking the '-' button in the same place. Once you are happy with your itinerary, you can click the 'Calculate Route' button to plot your route on the map:

Figure 2. A mapped tour

If you want to go back and edit your tour, click on the 'Plan Tour' tab above the map.
