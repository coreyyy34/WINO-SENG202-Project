The Vineyards menu allows you to access the Vineyards Info and Tour Planning pages.

Vineyard Info

Figure 1. The Vineyards screen

The Map View

Similar to the map view for wines, this map allows you to view the locations of vineyards on the map. The buttons in the top right corner will toggle the heatmap on and off.
