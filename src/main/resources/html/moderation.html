Review Moderation Features

You can use this screen to moderate reviews:

Figure 1. The review moderatin screen with a couple of flagged reviews

The table shows all of the reviews which have been flagged. Users can flag reviews by clicking on the 'Flag Review' button on the review on the detailed view page. The checkboxes on the right allow you to select reviews. The 'Delete' buttons will permanently remove the reviews, and the 'Keep' buttons will unflag them and leave them as-is.
