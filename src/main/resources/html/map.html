Figure 1. The Map Screen

  1. The Filters panel. This is the same as the one on the wines screen, and your filters will persist across these screens.

  3. The map buttons these allow you to toggle the markes and the heatmap on and off.

This is the mapping view. It shows where the wine comes from on the map. This feature is only available for wines which come from New Zealand. The wine glass icons will cluster together when they are close - The number on the icon represents the number of wines in this cluster.

Map Options

The buttons in the top right allow you to toggle the heatmap and icons on and off. The heatmap is a visual representation of where most of the wines come from.
